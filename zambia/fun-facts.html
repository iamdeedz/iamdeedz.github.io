Zambia!

Fun Facts

Here are some fun facts about Zambia!

  • The termite hills in Zambia are the size of a house
  • Zambia is home to all of Africa's Big Five Animals
  • The world's largest man-made lake, Lake Kariba, is in Zambia
  • The Unesco World Heritage Site and one of the Seven Wonders of the Natural World, Mosi-oa-Tunya a.k.a Victoria Falls is partly in Zambia
  • The first ever human heart transplant was done in Zambia
  • Zambia has a smaller population than North Korea despite being roughly six times larger
