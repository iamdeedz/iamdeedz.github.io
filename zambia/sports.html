Sports in Zambia

Football is the most popular and national sport of Zambia. Zambia have produced their fair share of athletic talent, with notable athletes including:

  • Samuel Matete, Olympic silver medalist
  • Joseph Musonda, 1 time Africa Cup of Nations Winner
  • Edgar Kazembe, Highest-ranking Zambian tennis player since 2008
  • Kabange Mupopo, African Championship silver medalist in 400m and Olympian
  • Kalusha Bwalya, African Footballer of the year 1988 and nominee for FIFA world player of the year 1996

Zambia's national football team won the Africa Cup of Nations in 2012 but have never qualified the World Cup Their national team is currently ranked 86th in the world by FIFA.
