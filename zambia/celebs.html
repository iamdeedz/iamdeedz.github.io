Zambia Celebrities

The list of famous Zambians stretches from composers to musicians to athletes. Here are some of the best at a glance.

  • Yo Maps, international artist
  • Denise Chaila, rapper and singer with Irish descent
  • Dambisa Moyo, author of 5 books, 4 of which are NY Times best sellers
  • Amon Simutowe, chess grandmaster, 3rd black grandmaster
  • Jonah Buyoya, multi-award winning journalist
