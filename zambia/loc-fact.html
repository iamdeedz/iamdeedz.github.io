Where is Zambia?

Zambia is a landlocked country in Southern Africa. It borders 8 countries. Angola to the west, the DRC to the north-west, Tanzania to the north-east, Malawi to the east, Mozambique to the south-east, Zimbabwe to the south and Namibia and Botswana to the south-west. It is 8,134km away from Ireland and is a nearly day-long trip from Dublin with the most common layover locations being Doha and Dubai.

Fact File

  • Capital City: Lusaka
  • Currency: Zambian Kwacha
  • Form of Government: Republic
  • Date Format: DD/MM/YYYY
  • Population: 19.61 million (2022)
  • Official Language: English
  • Other Languages Include: Bemba, Nyanja
  • Most Common Religion: Christianity
