Austrian Foods

  • Viennese Apfelstrudel. The Germans argue that the strudel is theirs, however the Austrians have claimed the Apfelstrudel as their own. It is listed among their national foods.

    Viennese Apfelstrudel

  • Wiener Schnitzel. Known also as the Vienna Schnitzel, the schnitzel is veal often served with fried potatoes and sour cream. It is also among their national foods.

    Wiener Schnitzel

  • Kaiserschmarrn. This desert is literally meant for kings. "Kaiser" means king and "Schmarrn" means shred. This dish is essentially fluffy bits of pancake.

    Kaiserschmarrn
