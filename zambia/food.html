Zambian Foods

  • Nshima. Nshima is a staple Zambian food. It is essentially a very thick porridge that is eaten with your hands and often served with meat and vegetables.

    Nshima

  • Ifisashi. Ifisashi is a dish made primarly from peanuts and greens. It is considered a vegetarian dish but meat can be added if desired. It is also often served with nshima.

    Ifisashi

  • Chikanda. Chikanda is made from the boiled root tubers from chikanda orchids. It is also served as an accompaniment to nshima.

    Chikanda
