History of Austria

The Republic of Austria was founded on the 13th of November, 1918, following Emperor Charles I stepping down.

Austria was annexed by Nazi Germany shortly before WWII and joined the axis powers (see more about Austria in WWII on the next page or click here). The republic was re-established after WWII and joined the European Union on the 1st of January, 1995.
