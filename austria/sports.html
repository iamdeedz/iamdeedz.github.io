Sports in Austria

Skiing is the most popular sport in Austria, followed by football and motorsports. Many a famous athlete has come from Austria including but not limited to:

  • Niki Lauda, 3 time Formula 1 world champion
  • Jochen Rindt, 1 time Formula 1 world champion.
  • David Alaba, football player for Real Madrid and previously Bayern Munich
  • Toni Sailer, won all three gold medals in alpine skiing at the 1956 Winter Olympics
  • Richard Bergmann, 7 time table tennis world champion and included in the International Table Tennis Foundation Hall of Fame

Austria's national football team has qualified for the FIFA World Cup 7 times and came in 3rd place in the 1954 World Cup. Their national team is currently ranked 24rd in the world by FIFA.

Austria have hosted the Winter Olympics twice, in 1964 and 1976. Both of these events were hosted in Innsbruck. They are also the leading country in alpine skiing by medal count.
