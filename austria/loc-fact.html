Where is Austria?

Austria is a landlocked country in Central Europe. It borders 8 countries. Slovenia to the south, Italy to the south-west, Switzerland and Liechtenstein to the west, Germany to the north-west, Czechia to the north and Slovakia and Hungary to the east.
