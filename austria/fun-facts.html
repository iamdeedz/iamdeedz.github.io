Fun Facts

Here are some fun facts about Austria!

  • The Austrian flag is one of the oldest flags in the world, dating back to the 12th century
  • The sewing machine was invented by an Austrian
  • Vienna has the oldest zoo in the world
  • The first postcards were used in Austria
  • 62% of Austria is covered by the Austrian Alps
  • Austria is home to the world's tallest waterfall. The Krimml Falls
  • Red Bull was created in Austria
