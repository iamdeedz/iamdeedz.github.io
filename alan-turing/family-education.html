Alan Turing

Birth and his Family

Alan Mathison Turing was born on the 23rd of June, 1912 in Maida Vale, London. He had one sibling, an older brother named John who was nearly 4 years older than him. Alan and John were fostered until 1926 while their parents were working in India.

Education

Alan attended Sherborne School, a boarding school in south-west England, from 1926 to 1931. From 1931-1934, he attended King's College, Cambridge, where he studied mathematics. After this, he went to Princeton University to earn his PhD in mathematical logic. This was completed in 1938.
